133 Montclair State University students responded to 15 items identified by Julia and Renata as relatively conforming to the Kuncel and Tellegen empirical patterns (linear, asymmetric, or quasi-symmetric). Two sample items were first presented (Make people feel welcome and Insult people). We retained responses if at least 75% of questions were answered, resulting in 116 student responses.
